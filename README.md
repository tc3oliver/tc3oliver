## 👋 About Me

I’m Oliver — a **Senior AI/ML Engineer** & **System Architect** based in Taiwan.  
I specialize in connecting **AI models**, **backend systems**, and **cloud infrastructure** —  
building end-to-end solutions that are scalable, reliable, and production-ready.

My career started in **mobile & web development**, evolved through **backend architecture** and **DevOps**,  
and now focuses on **AI/ML system integration**, **inference optimization**, and **data-driven automation**.
